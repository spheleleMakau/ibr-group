IBR Agriculture

IBR Agriculture operates across South Africa and Botswana, focusing on vegetable farming and livestock production. Despite challenges such as water scarcity in Botswana, the company continues to grow through strategic development and market engagement. Its South African operations have shown strong progress, supplying local markets and expanding production capabilities. The company’s goal is to optimize farming efficiency, improve output, and meet increasing market demand through sustainable agricultural practices.
