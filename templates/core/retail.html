IBR Retail

IBR Retail is a growing distribution and retail company that supplies a diverse range of products, including Ekhaya spices, sauces, beverages, and Tasty products. It also distributes clothing and agricultural products such as chickens to mining operations. The company plays a vital role in connecting various IBR businesses by bringing their products to market, with future plans to integrate more goods from IBR Agriculture and expand its footprint.
